About A2HackFest

A2HackFest is your gateway into the world of Agentic AI—the future of application development. Participants will use qRaptor, our next-gen Agentic App Builder, to create intelligent applications powered by AI agents.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just starting your tech journey, qRaptor makes it easy to build apps through drag-and-drop interfaces, vibe-code mode with intuitive prompts, and one-click deployment.

Idea Submission Guidelines

Phase: 11th Aug – 17th Aug 2025Results: 18th Aug 2025

Submission Requirements

Team Details

Team name, member information (names, emails), and contact details for communication

Project Title

A short, catchy name that clearly reflects your idea and captures attention

Problem Statement

Describe the real-world problem you're solving, including target users and industry context

Solution Overview

A high-level description of your idea, how it works, and the role of Agentic AI in your solution

qRaptor Integration

Explain how you plan to leverage qRaptor's capabilities like agents, orchestration, and data vault

Key Features

Bullet points or diagrams showing planned workflows and main functionalities (optional but recommended)

Expected Impact

Describe the potential benefits, value proposition, and scale of impact your solution offers

Demo Materials

Wireframes, mockups, or early demo links via Google Drive, Figma, or similar platforms (optional)

Tips for Strong Submission

1

Be original and specific in your problem statement - avoid generic solutions

2

Align your idea with Agentic AI principles and qRaptor's core features

3

Keep your write-up concise, clear, and well-structured for easy evaluation

4

Think about daily pain points in productivity, research, support, or learning

5

Avoid copying existing apps - add genuine innovation or AI-native elements

6

Research qRaptor's capabilities to showcase meaningful platform integration

7

Focus on feasibility - ensure your idea can realistically be built in the timeframe
